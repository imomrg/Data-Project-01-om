# 📘 Support Vector Machine (SVM)

## What is SVM?

SVM is a **Supervised Learning** algorithm used mainly for Classification.

It separates different classes using the best possible boundary.

---

## How does it work?

1. Learn labeled data.
2. Find the best separating line (boundary).
3. Predict the class of new data.

---

## Real-Life Example

Separate Apples and Oranges into two groups.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ High accuracy
- ✅ Works well on small datasets
- ✅ Good for classification

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Slow on very large datasets
- ❌ Difficult to understand

---

## Applications

- Face Recognition
- Spam Detection
- Disease Prediction

---
