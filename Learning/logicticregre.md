# 📘 Logistic Regression

## What is Logistic Regression?

Logistic Regression is a **Supervised Learning** algorithm used for **Classification** problems.

It predicts **categories** instead of numbers.

### Example

- Loan Approved / Rejected
- Spam / Not Spam
- Pass / Fail

---

## How does it work?

1. Learn from labeled data.
2. Find the relationship between input and output.
3. Predict the probability of each class.
4. Choose the most likely class.

---

## Real-Life Example

A bank predicts whether a customer's loan will be Approved or Rejected.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ Simple and fast
- ✅ Easy to understand
- ✅ Works well for binary classification

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Not suitable for complex data
- ❌ Can only learn simple relationships

---

## Applications

- Spam Detection
- Loan Approval
- Disease Prediction
