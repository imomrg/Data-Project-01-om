# 📘 Naive Bayes

## What is Naive Bayes?

Naive Bayes is a **Supervised Learning** algorithm based on Probability.

It predicts the most likely class using probabilities.

---

## How does it work?

1. Learn from labeled data.
2. Calculate probabilities.
3. Predict the most likely class.

---

## Real-Life Example

Email Spam Detection.

The model predicts whether an email is Spam or Not Spam.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ Very fast
- ✅ Easy to train
- ✅ Works well for text data

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Assumes features are independent
- ❌ Accuracy may decrease on complex datasets

---

## Applications

- Spam Detection
- Sentiment Analysis
- Text Classification
