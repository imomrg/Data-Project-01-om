# 📘 Decision Tree

## What is Decision Tree?

Decision Tree is a **Supervised Learning** algorithm that makes decisions by asking a series of questions.

It works like a flowchart.

---

## How does it work?

1. Start from the root.
2. Ask a question.
3. Follow the answer.
4. Continue until a final decision is reached.

---

## Real-Life Example

Should a loan be approved?

Income > ₹50,000?

↓

Yes

↓

Credit Score > 700?

↓

Approved

---

## Advantages

- ✅ Easy to understand
- ✅ Easy to visualize
- ✅ Handles both numbers and categories

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Can overfit
- ❌ Large trees become slow

---

## Applications

- Loan Approval
- Medical Diagnosis
- Fraud Detection
