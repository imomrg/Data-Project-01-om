# 📘 FP Growth Algorithm

## What is FP Growth?

FP Growth is an improved version of Apriori.

It finds frequent item combinations much faster.

---

## How does it work?

1. Build an FP Tree.
2. Find frequent patterns.
3. Generate association rules.

---

## Real-Life Example

Amazon recommending products based on purchase history.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ Faster than Apriori
- ✅ Efficient for large datasets

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ More complex to understand
- ❌ FP Tree construction takes memory

---

## Applications

- Product Recommendation
- Shopping Analysis
