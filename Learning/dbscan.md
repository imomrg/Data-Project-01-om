# 📘 DBSCAN

## What is DBSCAN?

DBSCAN groups data based on density.

It can also detect outliers.

---

## How does it work?

1. Find dense regions.
2. Form clusters.
3. Mark isolated points as outliers.

---

## Real-Life Example

Finding crowded places on a city map.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ Finds outliers
- ✅ No need to choose K

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Difficult parameter selection
- ❌ Doesn't work well for varying densities

---

## Applications

- GPS Data
- Fraud Detection
- Image Analysis
