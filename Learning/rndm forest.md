# 📘 Random Forest

## What is Random Forest?

Random Forest is a collection of many Decision Trees.

Instead of one tree making a decision,
many trees vote for the final answer.

---

## How does it work?

1. Create many Decision Trees.
2. Each tree predicts an answer.
3. Majority voting decides the final prediction.

---

## Real-Life Example

100 doctors check a patient.

The majority opinion becomes the final diagnosis.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ More accurate than one Decision Tree
- ✅ Reduces overfitting
- ✅ Handles large datasets

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Slower than Decision Tree
- ❌ Harder to understand

---

## Applications

- Loan Prediction
- Medical Diagnosis
- Fraud Detection
