# 📘 Hierarchical Clustering

## What is Hierarchical Clustering?

Groups similar data into a tree-like hierarchy.

---

## How does it work?

1. Treat every data point as one cluster.
2. Merge the closest clusters.
3. Repeat until one large cluster is formed.

---

## Real-Life Example

Creating a Family Tree.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ No need to choose K
- ✅ Easy to visualize

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Slow for large datasets
- ❌ High memory usage

---

## Applications

- Biology
- Customer Segmentation
- Document Clustering

---

## Quick Revision
