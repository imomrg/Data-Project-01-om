# 📘 K-Means Clustering

## What is K-Means?

K-Means is an **Unsupervised Learning** algorithm that groups similar data into K clusters.

---

## How does it work?

1. Choose K clusters.
2. Place cluster centers.
3. Assign data to the nearest center.
4. Repeat until groups stop changing.

---

## Real-Life Example

Grouping customers based on shopping habits.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ Simple
- ✅ Fast
- ✅ Easy to implement

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Need to choose K manually
- ❌ Sensitive to outliers

---

## Applications

- Customer Segmentation
- Image Segmentation
- Market Analysis
