# 📘 Apriori Algorithm

## What is Apriori?

Apriori is an Association Rule Learning algorithm.

It finds items that are frequently bought together.

---

## How does it work?

1. Find frequent items.
2. Generate rules.
3. Recommend related items.

---

## Real-Life Example

People who buy Bread also buy Butter.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ Easy to understand
- ✅ Useful for recommendations

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Slow on very large datasets
- ❌ Generates many rules

---

## Applications

- Market Basket Analysis
- Product Recommendation
