# 📘 Principal Component Analysis (PCA)

## What is PCA?

PCA is a Dimensionality Reduction algorithm.

It removes unnecessary features while keeping important information.

---

## How does it work?

1. Find important features.
2. Remove less useful features.
3. Create a smaller dataset.

---

## Real-Life Example

Compressing a large image without losing much quality.

---

## Advantages

- ✅ Faster model training
- ✅ Reduces dataset size
- ✅ Removes noise

---

## Disadvantages

- ❌ Information loss is possible
- ❌ Difficult to interpret

---

## Applications

- Data Compression
- Image Processing
- Data Visualization

---

## Quick Revision
